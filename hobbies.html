Hobbies

Things I enjoy outside of academics, coding, and career development

Outside of Computer Science

Outside of school and software development, I enjoy activities that help me stay creative, disciplined, and motivated. My hobbies give me a balance between problem solving, personal growth, and relaxing from academic work.

Interests

  • Technology I enjoy exploring new software tools, programming ideas, and technology trends. I like learning how different systems work and finding ways to apply them to my own projects.
  • Gaming I like games that involve strategy, creativity, and problem solving. Gaming also inspires some of the projects I have built, such as small browser games and arcade-style programs.
  • Fitness and Personal Growth I value staying active, building discipline, and improving myself over time. Fitness helps me stay focused and balanced while working toward my academic and career goals.
  • Creative Projects I enjoy building things, experimenting with design, and turning ideas into finished projects. Whether it is a website, game, or app, I like seeing an idea become something real.

Why These Matter

These hobbies help me become a better problem solver and developer. Technology keeps me curious, gaming strengthens my creativity and logic, fitness builds discipline, and creative projects help me practice turning ideas into real results.
