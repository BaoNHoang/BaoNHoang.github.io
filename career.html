Career

Bao’s career path blends hands-on technical support, software engineering, and project-driven development. Through a combination of academic learning and professional experience, he has built a foundation in computer science with a focus on user support and full-stack development.

IT Services Assistant — Christopher Newport University

At CNU, Bao worked in campus IT Services, where he gained experience managing enterprise user accounts through Azure Active Directory and LDAP supporting over 1,000 users across the university. He regularly resolved technical support tickets and provided onsite support developing strong communication and troubleshooting skills while ensuring compliance with state security policies. His role also included hardware lifecycle management, system administration, and working across Windows, Mac, and Linux environments.

Software Engineer Intern — Thomas Jefferson National Accelerator Facility

Bao expanded his technical expertise as a Software Engineer Intern at Jefferson Lab. He designed and implemented dynamic request forms in ColdFusion to digitize paper-based workflows and optimized SQL queries in Oracle Database to improve data retrieval efficiency. By working closely with end-users, he contributed iterative improvements that enhanced usability and reduced errors. His work digitizing physical records and ensuring secure authentication measures showcased his ability to balance technical development with real-world usability and data privacy needs.

Student Housing Assistant — Christopher Newport University

In his role with Student Housing, Bao supported both staff and contractors by ensuring safe and secure access to student living spaces. He assisted with preventative maintenance and logistical operations while also digitizing and organizing housing records. His contributions improved operational efficiency and reinforced the importance of clear coordination between technical and administrative teams.
